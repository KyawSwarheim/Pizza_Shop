Hawaiian


Price : 8000 Ks

Size: small

Strips for fast expansion or removal of pizzas from hot grills. They are wooden handles, with medium and large strips on the 11′′ edges. A peel is a tool that is like a shovel that bakers use to slide into the oven and out of a loaf of bread, pizzas, pastries and other baked products. It is generally fashioned of wood, with a flat supporting surface to hold the baked product and a handle from one side.
